01
Commercial real estate · Bangalore-first

Lokazen

Two-sided platform for brands that need space and owners that list it — matching, location intelligence, dashboards, and optional expert support.

  • Structured briefs, not random PDF dumps
  • Footfall, demographics, competition, accessibility signals
  • Brand Fit Index (BFI) & Property Fit Index (PFI)
  • Starter / Professional / Premium plans · PhonePe checkout
lokazen.in · Visual deck for social
02
Product

Two audiences, one stack

Same data model and scoring lens whether you’re hunting space or listing it.

Brands Natural-language or structured search → /filter/brand → curated matches → brand dashboard with map & intel panels → plans on /for-brands.
Owners Rich listing flow → /filter/owner → verified-style inventory → matched to serious operators by fit, not spam.
  • Hero search toggles Brand vs List Property modes on the homepage
  • Location intelligence preview page for area + category experiments
Public flows + logged-in dashboards (owner / brand / admin)
03
Brands

What brands get

  • AI-assisted search — messy brief → structured filters (query parsing + URL params to matching)
  • Brand dashboard — matches, requirements, fit bars, map (Google Maps), property intel & competitor context
  • Revenue-style scenarios — shown in-product with clear gating / honesty on guarantees
  • Category guidance — F&B, café, retail, wellness, services, cloud kitchen lenses on marketing pages
  • Plans — email vs WhatsApp vs dedicated manager, site visits, negotiation, legal on higher tiers
  • Payments — PhonePe integration for brand onboarding flows
Success fee messaging surfaced consistently on for-brands content
04
Owners

What owners get

  • Structured listing UX — property type (F&B + full taxonomy), Bangalore micro-markets, size sliders, rent, features, availability
  • Owner dashboard — pipeline view, property management, alignment with platform workflows
  • Matching — brands scored against inventory (PFI / mandate fit)
  • Same intelligence story — listings benefit from the same location & category thinking as brand-side
Onboarding & admin tooling in-app + API-backed persistence
05
Intelligence

Location intelligence layer

Rich geo schema in Postgres (wards, micro-markets, census-style attributes) powers APIs and UI copy — not just a lat/lng pin.

Footfall & mobility Pedestrian / peak signals, weekend lift — messaging aligned to Google & MapMyIndia-class data in product.
Demographics Income bands, age mix, catchment framing for spend fit.
Competition Category proximity, saturation, “whitespace vs data gap” style analysis in dashboard intel.
Accessibility Metro, buses, parking — operational reality for formats that need it.
  • Ward density & enrichment APIs (e.g. ward labels from coordinates)
  • Optional AI enrichment when Places data is thin (server-side prompts)
Schema: Prisma Location + related intelligence models
06
Scoring

BFI · PFI · charts

  • BFI (Brand Fit Index) — property ↔ brand mandate (size, budget, format, geography)
  • PFI (Property Fit Index) — brand ↔ asset economics & use-type fit
  • Recharts — bar, pie, area visualizations in dashboards for engagement & intel
  • Maps — React Google Maps, markers, heatmap layer where enabled; geo helpers for coords & property amenities
Revenue benchmarks & industry keys used in server-side calculators (typed TS)
