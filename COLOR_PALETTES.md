# Color Palette Options for N&G Ventures

## Option 1: **Midnight Blue & Teal** (Professional & Tech)
- **Background**: Deep navy blue (#0a1929) → Dark blue (#1a2332) → Teal (#0d3d56)
- **Primary**: Bright teal (#00d9ff)
- **Secondary**: Electric blue (#0066ff)
- **Accent**: Cyan (#00ffff)
- **Vibe**: Professional, trustworthy, tech-forward
- **Best for**: Corporate clients, enterprise solutions

## Option 2: **Deep Purple & Magenta** (Creative & Modern)
- **Background**: Dark purple (#1e0a3c) → Deep violet (#2d1b4e) → Dark magenta (#4a1942)
- **Primary**: Vibrant purple (#a855f7)
- **Secondary**: Hot pink (#ec4899)
- **Accent**: Bright magenta (#ff00ff)
- **Vibe**: Creative, bold, innovative
- **Best for**: Startups, creative agencies

## Option 3: **Forest Green & Emerald** (Natural & Luxurious)
- **Background**: Deep forest (#0a3d2c) → Dark green (#1a4d3d) → Deep teal (#0d4d4d)
- **Primary**: Emerald green (#10b981)
- **Secondary**: Jade (#14b8a6)
- **Accent**: Mint (#6ee7b7)
- **Vibe**: Sustainable, luxurious, premium
- **Best for**: Eco-conscious brands, premium properties

## Option 4: **Royal Blue & Gold** (Luxury & Elegance)
- **Background**: Deep navy (#0f1729) → Royal blue (#1e293b) → Dark slate (#1e3a5f)
- **Primary**: Royal blue (#3b82f6)
- **Secondary**: Gold (#fbbf24)
- **Accent**: Amber (#f59e0b)
- **Vibe**: Luxurious, elegant, prestigious
- **Best for**: High-end properties, luxury real estate

## Option 5: **Charcoal & Neon Lime** (Bold & Futuristic)
- **Background**: Almost black (#0a0a0a) → Charcoal (#1a1a1a) → Dark gray (#2a2a2a)
- **Primary**: Neon lime (#84cc16)
- **Secondary**: Electric green (#22c55e)
- **Accent**: Bright yellow (#facc15)
- **Vibe**: Bold, futuristic, energetic
- **Best for**: Modern brands, tech startups

- **Primary**: Coral (#fb923c)
- **Secondary**: Rose (#fb7185)

---

## How to Choose:

1. **Professional/Corporate** → Option 1 (Midnight Blue) or Option 8 (Ocean Deep)
2. **Creative/Bold** → Option 2 (Purple & Magenta) or Option 5 (Charcoal & Lime)
3. **Luxury/Premium** → Option 4 (Royal Blue & Gold) or Option 9 (Rose Gold)
4. **Natural/Eco** → Option 3 (Forest Green)
5. **Modern/Tech** → Option 7 (Cosmic Purple) or Option 1 (Midnight Blue)
6. **Warm/Inviting** → Option 6 (Sunset Warm) or Option 10 (Slate & Amber)
