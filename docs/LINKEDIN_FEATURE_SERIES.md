## Post 4 — BFI + PFI (single strong graphic)

**Visual:** Screenshot of homepage “Dual AI Scoring Engine” block (BFI + PFI cards) or a simple designed graphic with two columns.

**Copy:**

We score every relationship **from both sides**:

**BFI — Brand Fit Index** → how well a **property** matches a **brand’s** mandate (size, budget, format, geography).

**PFI — Property Fit Index** → how well a **brand** matches what the **asset** needs (use type, positioning, economics).

Same deal, two lenses — fewer wasted site visits.
